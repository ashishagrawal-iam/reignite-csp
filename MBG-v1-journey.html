MBG v1 — what we can ship now

The v1 build uses only two surfaces: CleverTap full-page in-app notifications for the big moments, and a single home banner with a drilldown screen for ongoing status. No changes to the assurance strip, chips, or home cards. The communication leads with the Minimum Business Guarantee (MBG) — branded to CSPs as कमाई कवच ("earnings shield"): cross the 60% install threshold and the month's earnings are guaranteed. It is not a "bonus" (that would imply unconditional extra). कमाई कवच is calculated each month and paid at month-end, running for 3 months (Jul–Sep). The 16th is not कमाई कवच — a separate, secondary quality bonus (quality-linked) pays on the 16th and is mentioned only in small, reassuring blocks. So every drilldown leads with कमाई कवच first, then a small quality-bonus reassurance.

🔔 CleverTap full-page notification

Full-screen takeover on Home (Home-allowlisted, as today). Hero + title + body + up to 2 CTAs. Used for: announcement, opt-in, mid-cycle reassurance, and payout day. Routes via the existing PendingRouteBus on CTA tap.

📢 Home banner → drilldown

One WiomBanner in the existing purple info style, placed just below the assurance strip on Home (server-driven BannerData). Tapping opens a dedicated Bonus drilldown screen — the only place state, numbers, and the 16-July reassurance live. Persistent, always available.

Out of scope for v1 (unchanged): the assurance strip & chips, the home task feed, the wallet screens, and any new home card. All states below are expressed purely through the two surfaces above.

Stage 1 · Announcement

The CSP hears about कमाई कवच (the MBG)

A full-page CleverTap takeover on Home — the biggest, clearest way to land the news within v1 limits.

Trigger: scheme launch / shortlistedCleverTap full-page
  • One-line promise up top (BV4): the CSP grasps it before reading anything else — "हर महीने ₹10,000 तक की कमाई पक्की" (the scheme is named कमाई कवच, never "बोनस").
  • The payout cadence is introduced here — कमाई कवच at month-end, the quality bonus on the 16th — so dates are anchored from the very first screen.
  • Two CTAs: "और जानें" (opens the drilldown) and "ठीक है" (dismiss). Both Home-routed via the existing in-app listener.
Why full-page, not a banner here: awareness needs maximum signal once. After this, the banner + drilldown carry the ongoing story — so the full-page is reserved for moments that genuinely deserve a takeover.

Stage 2 · Opt-in

The CSP accepts कमाई कवच

A second full-page framed as a mutual oath — each side makes one vow — and captures acceptance via the CTA payload. This is the Mutual Compact, realised inside CleverTap.

Trigger: taps "और जानें" / launch follow-upCleverTap full-page (accept CTA)
  • A mutual oath, not a one-way offer: each side makes one promise — Wiom vows weekly leads, the CSP vows 60% installs. "यही आपकी कमाई पक्की करता है." This two-way framing is what makes the commitment stick.
  • The guarantee as safety net: "कम कनेक्शन मिलें तो भी हर महीने ₹10,000 तक पक्के" — so the CSP's promise carries no downside risk.
  • Accept CTA "मैं वादा करता हूँ" — the button payload is captured by the existing CleverTap listener and recorded as opt-in (no new screen needed).
  • Plain terms below: ≥3 leads/month required, कमाई कवच paid at month-end.
Why the oath framing: a recorded mutual vow turns a broadcast into a relationship the CSP feels accountable to — the persistence the Cold Start floor demands. "मैं वादा करता हूँ" lands far stronger than "मैं तैयार हूँ".

Stage 3 · Guarantee active (daily)

The banner + drilldown become the home of the guarantee

From here on, the everyday story lives in one banner and one screen. This is the workhorse of v1 — the drilldown leads with the MBG (earnings guarantee), then reassures about the Bonus as a secondary add-on. That structure is identical in every state.

Trigger: opt-in recorded · dailyHome banner → drilldown
  • Banner (purple info, just below the assurance strip): calm and always-on — "इस महीने आपकी कमाई की गारंटी." Tapping opens the drilldown.
  • Drilldown leads with the MBG: a big, brand-tinted hero saying the month's earnings are guaranteed — do the work, the minimum is assured. This is the primary message.
  • Then the Bonus, secondary: a smaller block below — "और आपका क्वालिटी बोनस? 16 तारीख को · चिंता न करें" — so the Bonus is clearly an add-on, not the headline.
  • A status card makes both the amount and eligibility concrete: the ₹10,000 guarantee + earning so far + top-up, and an eligibility gauge showing install rate against the 60% target, with leads / installs / days-left — the same card the full journey shows on Home, here living inside the drilldown.
The whole v1 thesis: the CSP should never have to do bonus math or fear a cliff. The guarantee comes first (your earnings are safe), the quality bonus is a bonus (handled, pays on the 16th), and the only instruction is quality. Everything else Wiom computes server-side.

Stage 5 · Keep going

Below pace or few leads — honest, but never alarmed

v1's most important behavioural choice: we do not warn or threaten. But the make-good is gated — it pays only if month-end install rate is ≥60% and ≥3 leads were assigned. The drilldown shows the eligibility gauge (install rate vs the 60% target), so below pace reads as a calm recovery nudge ("finish your open leads to cross 60%"), and few leads (under 3) is shown as "not applicable — our fault", with install pay and the bonus kept safe.

Below pace gentle

A soft, non-red banner. Because the make-good needs ≥60% by month-end, this is an honest recovery nudge: finishing the open leads they already hold is what locks the guarantee in. Framed as opportunity ("गारंटी आपके हाथ में है"), never as risk.
