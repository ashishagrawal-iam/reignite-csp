Watch list — resolve before settlement

Risks the mechanism carries and open decisions still needed from leadership.

Cliff is a comms landmine risk

Case C (60% rate) earns 36% of Case B's payout for 60% of the work. Correct by design, but explosive when it hits a CSP's wallet. The near-miss nudge is the mitigation; show the cliff with eyes open, don't hide it.

Incremental value not yet provable risk

Dashboards prove spend and installs landed. They cannot yet prove installs were incremental (vs would-have-happened-anyway). Needs an untreated-network baseline wired before the Sep read, or burn answers "what we spent" but not "was it worth it."

Demand dependency risk

Density vs L* is the live constraint. The compact commits lead volume the demand team must actually deliver — a per-cluster commitment in writing should precede any signed compact, or every weak Sep result becomes a "wasn't our fault" debate.

Payout timing open

Cohort fully settles ~14 days after month-end (maturity tail). Confirm payouts are pushed by 14 days post-month.

Pro-ration basis open

Defaulting to ₹5,000 × remaining-days / days-in-month for mid-month signups. The ≥3-lead floor is held fixed (not pro-rated). This simulator assumes a full month — confirm both.

Gate boundary open

Encoded as ≥70% (7/10 qualifies, 6/10 does not). Confirm "≥" not ">".

Sample numbers are illustrative. Burn, distribution, and density figures must be sourced from Snowflake before any leadership decision. Lead maturity = 14 days; month = calendar month; settlement ~14 days post-month.
